01 — The Light

We begin with light.

Before a single object is placed, we study how the sun travels a room — where it pools in the morning, where it lengthens at dusk. Light is the first material, and the most honest one. Everything else is written around it.

  • 01 Solar mapping
  • 02 Layered ambient design
  • 03 Circadian warmth
02 — The Material

Then, material remembers.

Lime plaster that holds a fingerprint of light. Travertine cut to show its own weather. Oak that deepens with every season. We choose materials that age toward beauty rather than away from it — surfaces with a memory.

Limewash Travertine Smoked Oak Patinated Brass
